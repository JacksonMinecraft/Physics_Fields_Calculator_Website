SPH4U Fields Unit Calculator

Notes:

  • Enter the variables with numbered subscripts, from subscript 1 to 3 (m, q, r)
    • e.g. Use m1 before using m2 and m3
  • For question with two forces acting on a mass or charge, the force is acting on the third mass or charge
  • The angles are viewed the same way as in the CAST rule, from quadrant 1 to 4, and are all in degrees
  • Use the other two tabs to calculate other variables that are not on the magnetic fields tab
    • e.g. Calculate Fc in the electric fields tab and then sub that in for Fm in the magnetic fields tab
  • This website is not able to calculate variables for questions with more than one radius other than the force or field strength
    • e.g. For the equation g = G * (m1 / r12 + m2 / r22), it can calculate g, but not m1, r1, m2, or r2
